WE ARE WICKED PISSED!

A commentary, booklet, statement, and usable poster.

Protesting has always been lively in the city of Boston. This piece reflects the protests of the 21st century while also keeping in mind it's history of rebellion that holds a humanistic energy and fire set within the typography.

The three articles inside educate the audience about the protests that took place regarding a history of those in the Boston Common, ones in response to decisions by the Trump Administration, and a reflection on what American politics have become.
